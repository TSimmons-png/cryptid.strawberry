About Me

My name is Taylor aka cryptid.strawberry, I'm a twenty one year old mixed media self taught artist who has been drawing since they could pick up a pencil.
I create mixed media illustrations to make stories, experiment with my craft and spread joy by utilizing my lifelong passion in a variety of mediums to create whimsy.
I especially love doing character design and creating whismical illustrations. I am also working on my projects while trying to get my degree.
